Shipping Information

Haley Apparels proudly ships across Germany and select international destinations. Here's what you need to know:

  • Orders are dispatched within 1–3 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Standard shipping within Germany typically takes 2–5 business days.
  • International shipping times vary by destination and may take up to 14 business days.
  • Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on location and order weight.
  • Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking link via email.

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate. Haley Apparels is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect or incomplete information.

For questions or special delivery requests, contact us at info@haleyapparels.de.
